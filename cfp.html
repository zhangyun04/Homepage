📍 In conjunction with ICCV 2025 — Oct. 19–23, Honolulu, Hawai'i

📣 Call for Papers

We welcome short (4-page) and full-length (8-page) submissions on topics related to trustworthy foundation models and their applications.
Accepted papers will not be published in proceedings, allowing future resubmission.


Topics of interest include (but are not limited to):

  • 🧠 Machine unlearning for foundation models
  • 🧭 Bias discovery and mitigation in large-scale foundation models
  • 🛡️ Adversarial attacks and defenses in foundation models
  • 📏 Robustness and generalization in trustworthy foundation models
  • ⚖️ Fairness-aware training and evaluation
  • 🔍 Explainability and interpretability for large/black-box models
  • 🧪 Ethical concerns and responsible deployment
  • 📊 Benchmarks for foundation model trustworthiness
  • 📉 Uncertainty quantification
  • 🔐 Data provenance and security

🗓 Submission Deadline: August 1, 2025

📬 Notification: August 16, 2025
